Why UX Design and Usability Testing? Where do you see yourself within this nexus of UX design and product development?

Having experience in Usability Testing will open a few pathways for me in my field, should I choose to branch out. In medicine especially, website usability will be vital for patients to learn about their diseases and make contact with their physicians. I am also interested in medical appliances, such as different machines and apparatuses used for at-home treatment.







Where do you want your knowledge and skills to take you?

Overall, my interests lay in making medicine and everything difficult that comes with it easier for ordinary people to understand, creating an environment for doctors and patients to have trusting relationships. Humans are born with an ingrained fear of the unknown. Removing these unknowns and making communication between patients and their doctors has been shown to drastically increase in effectiveness of medical treatment. I want to take everything that I have worked with surrounding medicine and sciecne, and utilize it to help others like myself, who need to rely on their doctors.
